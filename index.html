History

The recorded history of chess goes back at least to the emergence of a
similar game, chaturanga, in seventh-century India.
Chess has a similar game that is an two player Indian war
game, Chatarung, which is dates back to 600 A.D.
In 1000 A.D, chess spread to Europe by Persian traders.
One of the first masters of the game was a Spanish priest named Ruy Lopez.

Top 7 Chess Stradegies

  • Occupy the center using the pawns
  • Develop your minor pieces
  • Don't forget to castle and remember your kings safety
  • Make a game plan
  • Occupy open and semi-open files with your rooks
  • Activate you king in endgame
  • Support/Attack Passed pawns from behind your rook
